# Code of Conduct

## Purpose
This Code of Conduct defines the standards of behavior expected from all employees, contractors, and collaborators of our company. It reflects our values and supports a safe, respectful, and ethical workplace.

---

## Our Core Principles

### 1. Integrity & Ethics
- Act honestly and transparently in all professional interactions.
- Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose them when they arise.
- Never engage in fraud, corruption, or deceptive practices.

### 2. Respect & Inclusion
- Treat everyone with dignity, respect, and fairness.
- We do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior of any kind.
- Value diverse perspectives and inclusive collaboration.

### 3. Accountability
- Take ownership of your work, decisions, and outcomes.
- Communicate clearly and proactively when issues arise.
- Learn from mistakes and focus on continuous improvement.

### 4. Security & Confidentiality
- Protect company, customer, and partner data at all times.
- Follow security best practices and internal policies.
- Do not share confidential or proprietary information without authorization.

### 5. Professional Conduct
- Maintain a professional, collaborative, and constructive attitude.
- Use company tools, systems, and resources responsibly.
- Avoid behavior that could harm the company, colleagues, or customers.

### 6. Compliance
- Follow all applicable laws, regulations, and internal policies.
- Respect intellectual property and licensing obligations.
- Cooperate with audits, investigations, and compliance efforts.

---

## Reporting & Enforcement
- Speak up if you observe behavior that violates this Code.
- Reports can be made to leadership or designated contacts without fear of retaliation.
- Violations may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

---

## A Shared Responsibility
This Code of Conduct applies to everyone. Living these principles every day helps us build a trusted product, a strong team, and a company we are proud of.
